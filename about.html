I am a senior Graphic Design student at WVU.
I was born and raised in Charleston, West Virginia and the Wild and Wonderful state has made me into the creative, and active person I am today. WV will always have my heart, but I love traveling.

Spring 2013 I studied abroad at Central St. Martins College of Art and Design in London, UK.

When I'm not designing or traveling, I love to ride my bike, run, hike, and scheming a new adventure. Last summer I biked across the country in the name of affordable housing for the non-profit, Bike & Build.
