I am a senior Graphic Design student at WVU. Let's go Mountaineers! I was born and raised in Charleston, West Virginia and the Wild and Wonderful state has made me into the creative, and active person I am today. Itâ€™s easy to find inspiration in a place like WV, it will always have my heart, but I also love to explore.

Spring of 2013 I attended a study abroad program at Central St. Martins College of Art and Design in London, UK. London has been the best experience of my life. Not only did I learn a tremendous amount of design theory. I was inspired beyond measure by the city, the art, and lifetime friends.

My latest adventure was Bike & Build. Summer 2014 I set off with 33 complete strangers in Virginia Beach with the mission to bike across the country to raise money and awareness for affordable housing. By the time we arrived at Cannon Beach, Oregon, it was 75 grueling days later, and those strangers become the greatest group of people I've ever met. It was an adventure of a lifetime. Bike & Build touched my heart, but also inspired my design and work ethic tremendously.
